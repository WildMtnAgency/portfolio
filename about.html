My Story

My journey in programming began with a curiosity for learning new things. As a self-taught developer, I have overcome many obstacles mainly choosing to be displined in my coding practice. By choosing consistency, I have learned how to create, maintain, troubleshoot, and host websites. Though as a developer my learning never ends, (that is why I love programming. I love learning!)Â I find so much fun in discovering new languages, methods, and finally troubleshooting that one feature. I was ready to learn something new and have more of a pathway into programming not as a hobbist or for friends, but as a career path. This is where I learned about Freemote. Freemote Bootcamp was the next right step in my coding journey. Not only did the bootcamp have a pathway and direction to become a programmer, but it had the right type of learning environment, all online. As I work full-time, I need a pathway that would work for me and my time. I loved the idea of help business owners thrive in the marketplace by selling their products through Shopify. Thanks to the Freemote bootcamp I am now living my dream of learning new things everyday and helping others thrive.
